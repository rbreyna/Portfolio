DayTrip Project

For my first group project in my bootcamp, my team and I built the DayTrip App. My role in this project was to make API calls to the Yelp! API and break the information we wanted. Users are able to look up things to do or places to eat/drink in any city in the United States! The top results (by rating) are shown after the search with the businesses address, contact info, and link to a Yelp! site. The current weather conditions for the city searched are displayed as well.
